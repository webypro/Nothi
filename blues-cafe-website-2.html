Our Story

This Is Our
Happy Place

Nestled above the city of Damoh on a breathtaking open rooftop, The Blues Cafe is where great food meets open skies and cinematic neon ambiance.

We serve 100% pure vegetarian multi-cuisine meals — sizzling Indian curries, authentic Chinese, wood-fired pizzas, indulgent waffles, and barista-style café beverages. Every dish is crafted with care and plated beautifully.
